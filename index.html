Dr. SAMUEL U. BENSON. PgD, Postdoc

Dr. Samuel U. Benson, a dynamic and accomplished professional, holds extensive expertise in strategic operations and business management. He has a diverse background encompassing agriculture, renewable energy, education, and entrepreneurship. Dr. Benson is particularly skilled in driving organizational and individual growth, sustainability, and efficiency through strategic planning, innovative solutions, excellent communication, and effective leadership.

Dr. Benson is currently serving as the Senior Operations Manager at Samovich Global Resources. In his previous roles, he demonstrated his prowess as a Business Development Manager at Andremovich Chemicals Limited, where he was recognized as the best manager of the year. His role as the Internal Auditor Manager at Watercress Hotel, also working alongside with the chief of staff which earned him accolades, including the best manager of the year award. Earlier in his career, he served as an Office Administrator Officer at Abax-OOSA Professionals and as a Portfolio Operations Officer in a hybrid capacity. Dr. Benson's academic credentials are equally impressive. He completed his postdoctoral studies in strategic management and leadership from the New York Learning Hub, NY, USA. He also holds an Advanced Certificate in Advanced Research Writing and Grant from the same institution. Currently, he is pursuing a Post Graduate Diploma (PGD) in Intellectual Property Law and an Advanced Certificate in Intellectual Property Strategy, both at the New York Learning Hub. Previously, he earned a Postgraduate Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership and an Advanced Certificate in Strategic Studies and Policy Implementation. Dr. Benson graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the University of Lagos, Lagos, Nigeria. His foundational education includes a high school diploma from Great Trinity College, Lagos, Nigeria. Additionally, he has undergone Modern Project Manager and Risk Training at Alison. As a researcher, Dr. Benson has made significant contributions to his field. His post-doctoral research publication, "Empowering Rural Africa: A Comprehensive Strategic Analysis of the Socioeconomic Benefits of Solar Energy Adoption," highlights his commitment to advancing knowledge and practical solutions in renewable energy. Dr. Benson's professional achievements and continuous pursuit of knowledge underscore his dedication to excellence and innovation.
